Self-Care.

Self-Care is a live HIIT (High Intense Interval Training) workout designed for busy moms of all levels. The classes are on Saturday and prepares you for the Mind Body program. The class is composed of restorative cardio, strength training, and core restoration to provide a complete a full body workout and to give you the energy and motivation you need to start a healthy lifestyle. This class is all about self-care to help you connect with other moms and feel energized and happy for the rest of your weekend.
