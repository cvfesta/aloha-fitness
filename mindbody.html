Mind Body.

Mind Body is a science-based nutrition program designed for busy moms by a mom to help women to reach a healthy life style. The program is designed to create healthy habits that will help you lose weight, stay fit, feel energized, and connect to other moms from home.

The live classes are HIIT (Hight Intensive Interval Training) composed of restorative cardio, strength training, and core restoration workout designed for moms of all levels. It provides a complete full body workout and gives you the energy and motivation you need to start a healthy lifestyle.

What you'll need:

Dumbbells

Light weight dumbbells for strength training.

Yoga Mat

A comfortable yoga mat for core training.

Jump Rope

A jump rope for cardio workouts.
